Resources for a more mindful and happier life!

  1. Calm app

    2. The calm app is a free guided meditation app that has a variety of relaxing sounds and backgrounds to match.

  2. Headspace app

    3. Similar to the calm app, the Headspace app is another meditation app that solely focuses on making one happier through meditation. The app teaches you everything you need to know about meditation in the first 10 days.

  3. Insight Timer app

    4. This app features over 4000 guided meditation and there are over 1000 instructors helping you throughout your meditation. There is the option of setting a timer to designate a specific amount of time for your meditation.

  4. Aura app

    5. Aura is a more peronalized app where a person received a new guided meditation daily and there are no repeats! This works best for those who do not like to follow a regular routine and want to switch things up to stay engaged!

  5. Stop, Breathe & Think app

    6. This app makes you get acquainted with the idea of mindfulness before you start going down the path through meditation. This one is difference because you first learn about mindfullness before working towards it, allowing the individual to have a greater understanding of happiness overall.
