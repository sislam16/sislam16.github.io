Mind>Matter

When underestimating one's willpower becomes a thing of the past. we are stronger than we know and it is up to us to unleash the strength we always had to be the best version of ourselves. This webpage will inform you about the power of our minds and help you re-gain control of your thoughts and happiness!

To begin our mindfullness journey make sure to practice the following:

Awareness

Embrace all of the feelings that you are experiencing, dont disregard any emotion!

Be Present

Acknowledge what you are feeling RIGHT NOW! How is your body feeling? What's going through your mind?

No Judgement

Try to remove any negative emotion! Acknowledge the feeling but don't categorize it as something bad.

Kindness

Be patient with yourself, be gentle and be friendly with yourself! There's enough going on externally, you don't need to be so hard on yourself!
