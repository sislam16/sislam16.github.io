New York City!

New York City, also known as NYC and the City of New York, is considered the cultural, financial and media capital of the world. It is home to the United Nations headquarters, Wall Street, New York Stock Exchange, NASDAQ, Metropolitan Museum of Art, the Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, Times Square, Broadway theater district, Central Park, Fifth, Park and Madison Avenues, New York Yankees and Mets Stadiums, Madison Square Garden, Columbia and New York Universities, and many many more of landmarks and attraction sites. With an area of about 303 square miles and a population of about 8,622,700 people, New York City is ranked number one most populous city in the United States. It also has the largest foreign-born population among all other metropolitan areas in the world. Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, The Bronx and Staten Island are the five boroughs, of which New York City consists. Each of these five boroughs is a county of the State of New York.
