WELCOME TO
STATEN ISLAND

Staten Island, also known as “The Boogie Down Bronx” has the bad rap as being the most dangerous borough of New York. One need not be scared of visiting the Bronx, it is a vibrant, bustling community of approximately 1,500,000 inhabitants and has plenty to see and do. The Bronx has 24% parkland, a percentage larger than any other borough. The Bronx is the only borough that is connected to the mainland. It is the birthplace of people such as rapper of the year Cardi-B, world renowned scientist Neil deGrasse Tyson, Junior Supreme Justice Sonia Sotomayor, and 80's pop singer Billy Joel. The musical genre Hip Hop that is now popular all over the world started in The Bronx. So come to The Bronx to visit the “real” Little Italy for food, see animals in the Bronx Zoo, enjoy nature of the New York Botanical Garden, view fine art in The Bronx Museum of the Arts, City Island for seafood, feel the sun and sand of Orchard Beach, and much, much more.
